Pulao:

Description:

Pulao is a simpler rice dish compared to biryani. It's made by cooking rice with vegetables or meat and mild spices, resulting in a subtle and aromatic flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup vegetables (peas, carrots, beans) or meat (chicken, mutton)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Whole spices (cumin seeds, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Turmeric and garam masala for flavor
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Wash and soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a pot, heat oil or ghee and add the whole spices.
  3. Add chopped onions, ginger, and garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add vegetables or meat and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add turmeric and garam masala, and then the drained rice.
  6. Pour in water or broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
