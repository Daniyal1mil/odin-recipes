Biryani:

Description:

Biryani is a popular Indian and South Asian dish known for its fragrant and flavorful rice preparation. It's made with aromatic spices, rice, and either chicken, mutton, or vegetables, and is often garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, mutton, or vegetables
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods, cloves)
  • Biryani masala (a mix of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder)
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves for garnish
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk for coloring (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Wash and soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and add the whole spices. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown. Remove some for garnish.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the meat or vegetables and brown them.
  6. Add biryani masala, chopped tomatoes, and yogurt. Cook until the oil separates.
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat/vegetable mixture.
  8. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, and fresh herbs over the rice.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is cooked and fluffy.
